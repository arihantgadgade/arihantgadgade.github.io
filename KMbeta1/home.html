How to Use:

Create a mindmap using the Map Maker, or click on one of the Maps in the Map Repository.

Desktop: Scroll to enlarge/minimize the graphs. Click and drag to move around

Mobile: Can use pinch to enlarge/minimize the graphs. Drag to move around

*** Some of the larger graphs (like KMver4) you may need to scroll first before being able to see
