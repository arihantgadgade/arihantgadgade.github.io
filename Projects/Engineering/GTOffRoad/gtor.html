2.3. PI Training üîù

I did the Prototyping Instructor Training at the Invention Studio. Here I learned how to use various machines and tools. This was useful for GTOR as the trainings for the CMA are much more bottlenecked by training requirements and trainers.

Also included is casting training, as you can get trained on various other tools and equipment.

Note:
- GTOR is located in the SCC (Student Competition Center)
- Invention Studio is in another building on campus
- Training certifications are not mutually recognized, I'm doing it for the knowledge and skills. Also if I bring material to the Invention Studio I can use the tools there to manufacture and bring the finished piece back to the SCC if necessary.
