Writings

Articles are ordered two ways: chronologically and categorically.

  • Chronologically from oldest to newest. I prefer that to generally how blogs/substacks show the newest writings. I like building up the writer/creator in my head from their oldest to newest writings.
  • Categorically they are given a definite category as opposed to being tagged with multiple categories, I prefer this just for the visual categorical structure.
